### About Me

Hi! I am a first-year M.S. student in [Computational Science and Engineering](https://cse.gatech.edu/) at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I earned my B.S. from [Xingjian College](https://www.xjc.tsinghua.edu.cn/), Tsinghua University in 2025, where I was advised by [Prof. Yanan Sui](https://yanansui.com) at the [LNS Group](https://lnsgroup.cc).

My research centers on the **alignment of large language models**. I study *what computation actually happens inside a model* — **mechanistic interpretability** — and *how to steer that computation safely* — **safety alignment**. I am equally captivated by **AI agents and the harness** we build around them. I think of all of this as the **neuroscience of LLMs**: reverse-engineering an artificial mind in order to understand and align it.
